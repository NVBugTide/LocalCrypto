Frequently asked questions

Is anything uploaded to a server?

No. All encryption and decryption happens locally in this tab using the Web Crypto API. Once the page is loaded, LocalVault is designed to work offline.

What happens if I lose my password?

The encrypted data cannot be recovered.

Can I check or host the code myself?

Yes. LocalVault is written in plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You can view the source, inspect it and host your own copy.

Is this suitable for very large files?

Depending on your RAM and browser.
